BLOG

Today we live in a world overflowing with the data that must be managed. Every moment of our lives is turning into a chunk of data. Whether we are at home chatting, outside traveling, or shopping, there is always some information being generated. In other words, every day brings forth new types of information, varying from person to person. To manage, interpret, and research such data, we need a new kind of science, and that is where data science comes in. With this new science, we are not only able to fully understand the redundancy of data but also learn how to clean, interpret, and store it for a long term.

In the beginning, people thought of this science as a small field because we didn't have as much data as we do today. However, over time, new fields within data science emerged, such as data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and data engineering. consequently, we now have AI, thanks to the early development of data science.
